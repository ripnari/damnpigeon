Before you sign up

Do I need any experience? +

No. Module 01 starts from zero — if you've never touched a sewing machine, you're in the right place. Later modules build on what you learn, so taking them in order is the easy path.

Do I need my own sewing machine? +

Not for the in-studio classes — the machines and tools are there for you. If you want to keep building at home afterward, you'll get a straight recommendation on what to buy and what to skip.

Where do classes happen? +

In the studio on Slauson Ave. In-person, small groups, hands-on. Remote and self-paced options are in the works for the modules that make sense for it.

What do I walk out with? +

Something you made. Module 01 sends you home with a finished hoodie and the know-how to make the next one without help. Every module is built around producing a real result, not just notes.

How much does it cost? +

The Hoodie Class is priced per session — current pricing is on the booking page. Full-curriculum bundles and mentorship packages are coming as the rest of the modules go live. Join the waitlist to hear pricing first.

Can I just do the one class? +

Absolutely. Start with the Hoodie Class and stop there if that's all you need. The curriculum is there if you catch the bug and want to take it all the way to your own brand.
